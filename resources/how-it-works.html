How It Works

Up and Running in Minutes, Not Months

3 simple steps to transform your property operations. No complex migrations, no lengthy onboarding — just results from Day 1.

⚙️

Step 1

Sign Up & Configure

Get your account ready in just 10 minutes.

📝

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email and verify your identity. No credit card required for the free trial. You'll be in your dashboard in under 60 seconds.

🏢

Set Up Your Company

Add your company details, logo, and trade license information. Configure your business structure — single entity or multi-branch operations across the GCC.

⚙️

Configure Property Types

Choose your property types — residential, commercial, mixed-use, or all of the above. Set up unit categories, rent structures, and VAT settings for your market.

Step 2

Import Your Data

Bring your existing portfolio into Darsync in about 30 minutes.

🏠

Import Properties & Units

Upload your property list via CSV or Excel. Darsync auto-detects columns and maps your data. Or add properties manually with our guided wizard.

👥

Import Tenants & Owners

Bring in your tenant records, owner details, and contact information. Emirates ID, passport numbers, and visa details are all supported.

📄

Import Leases & Contracts

Upload active leases with rent amounts, payment schedules, and PDC details. Darsync automatically calculates upcoming renewals and escalations.

Supported formats: CSV, XLSX, XLS — or use our API for direct integration with your existing systems.

Rent reminders, lease expiry alerts, compliance deadlines, and maintenance scheduling — all automated from the moment you go live.

Team Gets Access

Invite your team with role-based permissions. Property managers, accountants, maintenance staff, and leasing agents each see only what they need.

Common Questions

Onboarding FAQ

How long does the full setup take?

Most customers are fully operational within 1–2 hours. The initial configuration takes about 10 minutes, data import takes 30 minutes for a typical portfolio, and team onboarding can happen over the first few days.

Can I import data from my current system?

Yes. Darsync supports CSV and Excel imports with auto-detection of data formats. We can also do direct API integrations with popular systems. Our onboarding team can assist with complex migrations at no extra cost.

Do I need technical knowledge to set up Darsync?

Not at all. Darsync is designed for property managers, not IT teams. The setup wizard guides you through every step, and our onboarding specialist is available to help if you get stuck.

What if I manage properties across multiple emirates or countries?

Darsync supports multi-entity, multi-country operations out of the box. You can manage properties across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Oman, Qatar, and Kuwait from a single dashboard with country-specific compliance rules.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every plan comes with a free 14-day trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. Import your real data and see results before you commit.
