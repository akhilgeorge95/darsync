Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about Darsync property management software for the UAE and GCC.

General

Darsync is a comprehensive property management software platform built specifically for the UAE and GCC market. It covers the full property lifecycle — from leasing and rent collection to maintenance, compliance, and financial reporting — with native support for Ejari, PDC management, VAT compliance, and Arabic language. With 303+ features, it's designed to replace spreadsheets, disconnected tools, and manual processes with a single unified platform.

Darsync is built for property management companies, real estate developers, facility management firms, and individual landlords managing portfolios across the UAE and GCC. Whether you manage 10 units or 10,000, Darsync scales to fit your operations. Our users include property managers, finance teams, maintenance coordinators, leasing agents, and owners/investors.

Darsync is purpose-built for the Gulf market. Unlike generic property management tools adapted from Western markets, we offer native Ejari/Tawtheeq integration, PDC lifecycle automation, RERA-compliant rent indexing, Arabic-first design with full RTL support, WhatsApp business workflows, and multi-currency support for AED, SAR, BHD, OMR, QAR, and KWD. Every feature is designed around how property management actually works in the GCC.

Yes. Darsync features full Arabic language support with right-to-left (RTL) interface design. All tenant communications, invoices, contracts, and reports can be generated in Arabic or English. You can switch languages at any time, and different team members can use the platform in their preferred language simultaneously.

Darsync supports property management operations across the entire GCC region: UAE (all seven emirates including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah), Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Oman, Qatar, and Kuwait. Each country has its own compliance rules, tax settings, and regulatory frameworks pre-configured in the system.

Pricing & Billing

You pay a monthly fee per active user on your account. Each user gets full access to the features included in your plan. Tenant and owner portal users are completely free and do not count toward your user limit. This means your tenants and owners can access their portals, pay rent, submit requests, and view reports without adding to your costs.

All plans include core property management features: property and unit management, tenant management, lease tracking, rent collection, maintenance work orders, and basic reporting. Higher-tier plans add advanced automation, Ejari/Tawtheeq integration, PDC management, WhatsApp workflows, API access, custom reporting, dedicated account management, and priority support. Visit our pricing page for a full feature-by-feature comparison.

Yes. You can change your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no penalties, cancellation fees, or lock-in contracts.

Security & Compliance

Yes. Darsync uses bank-grade encryption — AES-256 for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We implement role-based access controls, two-factor authentication (2FA), IP whitelisting, session management, and audit logging. Our infrastructure undergoes regular third-party security audits and penetration testing to ensure your data is protected to the highest standards.

Yes. Darsync is fully GDPR compliant and also adheres to UAE data protection regulations under the Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL). We provide data processing agreements (DPA), data export tools, consent management, and right-to-deletion workflows to ensure you meet your regulatory obligations.

Darsync data is hosted on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with primary data centers located in the UAE, ensuring full data residency compliance. We maintain 99.9% uptime SLA with automated daily backups, geographic redundancy, and a comprehensive disaster recovery plan. Your data never leaves the region.
