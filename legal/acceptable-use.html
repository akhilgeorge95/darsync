1. Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs the use of Darsync's property management software platform and all related services (the "Service"). This AUP is incorporated into and forms part of our Terms of Service.

By using the Service, you agree to comply with this AUP. We may update this policy from time to time, and your continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any changes. If you violate this AUP, we may suspend or terminate your access to the Service as described in Section 7.

2. Prohibited Uses

You may not use the Service to:

2.1 Illegal Activities

  • Engage in or facilitate any activity that violates applicable local, national, or international law, including UAE federal laws and regulations
  • Process, store, or transmit data that you do not have the legal right to collect or use
  • Facilitate money laundering, terrorist financing, or sanctions evasion
  • Engage in fraudulent property management activities or misrepresentation

2.2 Security Violations

  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Service, other accounts, systems, or networks connected to the Service
  • Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Service or any related infrastructure without prior written authorization
  • Circumvent, disable, or interfere with security features of the Service
  • Introduce viruses, worms, Trojan horses, or other malicious code
  • Perform denial-of-service attacks or overwhelm the Service infrastructure

2.3 Abuse of the Platform

  • Use the Service for purposes other than property management operations
  • Scrape, harvest, or extract data from the Service using automated means without authorization
  • Reverse engineer, decompile, disassemble, or attempt to derive the source code of the Service
  • Resell, sublicense, or provide access to the Service to third parties outside of your organization without Darsync's written consent
  • Use the Service to develop a competing product or service
  • Misuse the customer support system by submitting excessive, duplicate, or abusive requests

2.4 Communication Abuse

  • Send unsolicited bulk messages (spam) through the Service's communication features
  • Use Darsync's email or messaging features for marketing purposes unrelated to legitimate property management communications
  • Impersonate another person or entity in communications sent through the Service

3. Usage Limits

To ensure fair use and optimal performance for all customers, the following usage limits apply:

If you exceed these limits, we will notify you and work with you to adjust your usage or upgrade your plan. Sustained overuse without resolution may result in service throttling.

4. Account Sharing Policy

Darsync licenses are issued on a per-user basis. The following rules apply:

  • Individual Accounts: Each user must have their own unique login credentials. Sharing login credentials between individuals is strictly prohibited.
  • No Concurrent Sessions: A single user account may not be used simultaneously from multiple devices or locations. Concurrent session detection is in place and may trigger automatic logout.
  • Team Changes: When a team member leaves your organization or changes roles, their account should be deactivated and a new account created for their replacement. User accounts are not transferable between individuals.
  • Read-Only Access: If you need to provide limited access to stakeholders (e.g., property owners viewing reports), use the dedicated Owner Portal or Viewer roles rather than sharing user accounts.
  • Third-Party Access: If you need to grant access to third-party contractors or consultants, contact support to set up appropriately scoped guest accounts.

5. API Usage Limits

Darsync provides API access for integrations with third-party systems. The following rate limits apply:

Metric Standard Enterprise
Requests per minute 60 300
Requests per hour 1,000 10,000
Requests per day 10,000 100,000
Bulk operations per request 100 records 1,000 records
Webhook endpoints 10 50

API responses include rate limit headers (X-RateLimit-Limit, X-RateLimit-Remaining, X-RateLimit-Reset) so your applications can manage requests accordingly. Exceeding rate limits will result in HTTP 429 (Too Many Requests) responses.

If your integration requires higher limits, contact us to discuss custom API plans.

6. Content Standards

All content uploaded to or created within the Service must comply with the following standards:

  • Must be accurate and not intentionally misleading
  • Must not contain hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory content
  • Must not infringe on the intellectual property rights of third parties
  • Must not contain personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary for property management operations
  • Must comply with applicable data protection laws regarding data of tenants, owners, and other individuals

7. Consequences of Violation

Violations of this AUP may result in the following actions, depending on the severity and nature of the violation:

  1. Warning: For first-time, minor violations, we will issue a written warning via email with a description of the violation and instructions for remediation. You will have 7 days to correct the issue.
  2. Temporary Suspension: For repeated violations or moderate severity violations, we may temporarily suspend access to the Service while the issue is investigated and resolved. We will notify you of the suspension and the steps required to restore access.
  3. Feature Restriction: We may restrict specific features (e.g., API access, email sending) that are being misused while allowing continued access to the rest of the Service.
  4. Permanent Termination: For severe violations (security breaches, illegal activity, or continued violations after warnings), we may permanently terminate your account. You will be provided with a 30-day window to export your data before deletion.

In cases of illegal activity or imminent threat to the Service or other users, Darsync reserves the right to take immediate action without prior notice, including suspending access and reporting the activity to relevant authorities.

8. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any activity that violates this AUP, please report it immediately:

  • Email: abuse@darsync.ae
  • Support: Contact our support team via live chat or phone

All reports are taken seriously and will be investigated promptly. We respect the confidentiality of individuals who report violations in good faith.
