Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent billing practices.

1. 14-Day Free Trial

Darsync offers a 14-day free trial to all new customers. During the trial period:

  • No payment information required: You can start your trial without entering any credit card or payment details.
  • Full feature access: You will have access to all features of your selected plan so you can evaluate Darsync thoroughly.
  • No automatic charges: Your trial will not automatically convert to a paid subscription. You must actively choose to subscribe at the end of the trial period.
  • Data preserved: If you subscribe at the end of your trial, all data entered during the trial will be preserved. If you choose not to subscribe, your data will be retained for 30 days and then permanently deleted.

2. Cancellation Process

You can cancel your Darsync subscription at any time through one of the following methods:

  1. Self-Service: Log in to your Darsync account, navigate to Settings > Subscription > Cancel Subscription, and follow the on-screen instructions.
  2. Email: Send a cancellation request to billing@darsync.ae from the email address associated with your account.
  3. Support: Contact our support team at +971 50 761 2213 during business hours (Sunday-Thursday, 9 AM - 6 PM GST).

Upon submitting a cancellation request, you will receive a confirmation email within 1 business day. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

3. Refund Eligibility

3.1 Annual Subscriptions

If you cancel an annual subscription before the end of your billing period, you are eligible for a pro-rata refund of the unused portion of your subscription:

  • The refund is calculated based on the number of full months remaining in your annual term.
  • The monthly rate used for the pro-rata calculation will be the standard monthly rate (not the discounted annual rate).
  • If the amount already consumed at the monthly rate exceeds the annual fee paid, no refund will be issued.

Example: If you paid AED 12,000 for an annual plan (AED 1,000/month) and cancel after 4 months, and the standard monthly rate is AED 1,400/month, the consumed amount is AED 5,600. Your refund would be AED 12,000 - AED 5,600 = AED 6,400.

3.2 Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions are not eligible for refunds. When you cancel a monthly subscription:

  • Your access continues until the end of the current monthly billing period.
  • No further charges will be applied after cancellation.
  • No partial refund is provided for the remaining days in the current billing period.

3.3 Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Setup fees or onboarding charges (if applicable)
  • Custom development or integration work
  • Training sessions that have already been delivered
  • Third-party add-on costs billed through Darsync

4. How Refunds Are Processed

Once a refund is approved:

  • Processing time: Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days from the date of approval.
  • Refund method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the subscription. If the original payment method is no longer available, we will work with you to arrange an alternative method (e.g., bank transfer).
  • Currency: Refunds are issued in the same currency as the original payment.
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed, including the refund amount and estimated arrival date.

5. Data Handling After Cancellation

We understand that your data is important. After cancellation, we provide a clear data handling process:

Timeline Action
Day 0 (Cancellation) Cancellation confirmed. Full access continues until the end of the billing period.
End of Billing Period Account moves to read-only mode. You can log in and export your data but cannot create new records.
30 Days After End of Billing Data Export Window closes. All customer data is permanently deleted from active systems.
90 Days After End of Billing Data purged from all backup systems. Deletion is irreversible.

Data Export: During the 30-day export window, you can export your data in standard formats (CSV, PDF) through your account settings. If you need assistance with data export, our support team is available to help.

6. Exceptions & Special Circumstances

We may consider refund requests outside of the standard policy in the following circumstances:

  • Service failure: If Darsync experiences extended downtime or service failures beyond the commitments in our SLA, you may be eligible for service credits or a refund.
  • Billing errors: If you were charged in error (e.g., duplicate charge, incorrect amount), we will issue a full refund for the erroneous charge immediately.
  • Regulatory requirements: If local consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction mandate a different refund policy, we will comply with the applicable legal requirements.
