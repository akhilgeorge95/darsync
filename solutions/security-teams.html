The Problem

Security Still Runs on Paper in 2026

While every other department has gone digital, security teams are still stuck with clipboards, paper logs, and radio calls.

📓

Paper-Based Visitor Logs

Handwritten visitor registers that are illegible, unsearchable, and easily tampered with. If you need to find who visited a building six months ago, good luck flipping through pages of scrawled names.

🔑

Untracked Access Cards

No centralized record of who has which access cards, keys, or remote controls. When a tenant moves out, there is no reliable way to ensure all access credentials are returned and deactivated.

🚨

No Incident Reporting System

Security incidents are reported verbally or in handwritten notes that get lost. No photos, no timestamps, no follow-up tracking. When management or authorities ask for records, there is nothing reliable to show.

Key Features

Security Operations, Fully Integrated

Visitor QR Registration

Self-service visitor check-in via QR code. Pre-registered guests get express entry. Real-time visitor count per building. Full digital log searchable by name, date, or host unit.

Gate Logging

Digital gate log for all entries and exits — visitors, deliveries, contractors, and service providers. Time-stamped records with vehicle details and photo capture for complete accountability.

Patrol Checkpoints

NFC or QR-based checkpoint scanning on patrol routes. Real-time tracking of patrol completion. Automated alerts for missed or late checkpoints. Historical patrol compliance reports.
