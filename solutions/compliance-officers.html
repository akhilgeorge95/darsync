The Problem

Compliance at Scale Is Impossible Manually

When you are responsible for hundreds of leases across multiple authorities, manual tracking is not just inefficient — it is risky.

📚

Manual Tracking Across Hundreds of Leases

Ejari registrations, Tawtheeq certificates, RERA filings, trade licenses, tenant visas, insurance policies — each with different renewal dates and requirements. Spreadsheets cannot scale.

⚠️

Document Expiry Nightmares

Tenant passports, Emirates IDs, trade licenses, and insurance certificates all expire on different dates. A single expired document can invalidate an entire lease and create legal liability.

💸

Fines and Legal Exposure

Missed Ejari renewals mean fines. Late RERA filings mean penalties. Unregistered leases mean unenforceable contracts. The cost of non-compliance far exceeds the cost of prevention.
