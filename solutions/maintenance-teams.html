The Problem

Maintenance Without Visibility Is Chaos

When your team is in the field without the right tools, every repair takes longer than it should.

👁️

No Visibility Into Workload

Managers cannot see who is available, who is overloaded, or where technicians are. Work orders pile up unevenly while some team members sit idle. No real-time status means constant phone calls.

⏱️

Missed SLAs

Without automated tracking, urgent requests get buried under routine tasks. Tenants wait days for fixes that should take hours. SLA violations damage your reputation and trigger penalty clauses.

🚘

Manual Dispatch Wastes Hours

Phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and handwritten notes to coordinate technicians. No route optimization means your crew drives past properties they should be visiting. Time on the road instead of on the job.

Key Features

Built for the Field, Not Just the Office

Mobile-first tools designed for technicians who spend their day fixing things, not filling out forms.

Auto-Assignment

Intelligent work order routing based on technician skills, current location, workload, and SLA urgency. The right person gets the right job without manager intervention.

SLA Tracking
