The Problem

Your Month-End Shouldn't Take a Month

Property finance in the UAE comes with unique challenges that generic accounting software simply cannot handle.

💳

PDC Management Chaos

Hundreds of post-dated cheques across multiple tenants and properties. Tracking deposit dates, clearances, bounces, and replacements in spreadsheets is a recipe for missed deposits and lost revenue.

📑

Manual Reconciliation

Matching bank transactions to invoices one by one. Hunting for discrepancies across multiple bank accounts and property ledgers. A single error cascades through every owner statement.

💸

VAT Compliance Headaches

UAE VAT on commercial properties, exemptions on residential, partial claims on mixed-use — getting it wrong means FTA penalties. Manual VAT calculations across hundreds of invoices invite errors.
