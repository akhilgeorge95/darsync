The Problem

Leasing Should Not Feel Like Herding Cats

Most leasing agents lose deals not because of bad properties — but because of slow, disorganized processes.

📓

Lead Tracking in Notebooks

Inquiries come from portals, walk-ins, referrals, and social media. Without a central system, leads fall through the cracks. You forget to follow up and the tenant signs somewhere else.

🐢

Slow Lease Processing

Manual contract drafting, printing, signing, scanning, and filing. Each lease takes days instead of hours. Meanwhile, hot prospects cool off and choose faster-moving competitors.

🔍

No Pipeline Visibility

How many leads are in your pipeline? Which showings converted? What is your average time-to-lease? Without data, you cannot optimize your process or prove your value to owners.
