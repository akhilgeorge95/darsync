The Challenges

Why Student Housing Is Uniquely Demanding

Student housing operates on a completely different rhythm than traditional residential, with unique operational requirements that generic property software cannot handle.

📅

Semester Turnover

Unlike traditional residential with year-long leases, student housing turns over twice yearly. Each semester end triggers hundreds of simultaneous move-outs, inspections, cleaning, repairs, and move-ins, all within a 2-3 week window.

👥

Roommate Matching

Pairing students by preferences, study habits, nationality, and programme creates a matching puzzle that grows exponentially with scale. Poor matches lead to complaints, room swaps, and early terminations that drain revenue.

👪

Parent Communication

Parents are often the ones paying rent and want visibility into their child's accommodation. Managing a separate communication channel for parents, with appropriate privacy boundaries, adds complexity to every interaction.

📈

Seasonal Demand Spikes

Demand peaks before each semester and drops during holidays. Managing pricing, availability, and marketing on seasonal cycles requires dynamic tools that most property managers lack.
