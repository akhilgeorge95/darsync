The Challenges

Why Residential Property Management Is So Hard

Managing residential properties in the UAE comes with unique challenges that spreadsheets and generic software simply cannot handle.

🔄

High Tenant Turnover

Residential properties in Dubai see average annual turnover of 25-30%. Every move-out triggers inspections, deposit returns, Ejari cancellation, relisting, and new tenant onboarding. Without automation, each turnover eats 8-10 hours of admin time.

💰

Rent Collection at Scale

Managing PDC (post-dated cheque) schedules across hundreds of units, tracking bounced cheques, sending reminders, and reconciling payments manually is a full-time job. Late payments cascade into cash flow nightmares for owners.

🔧

Maintenance Backlogs

Residential tenants expect fast response times. But without SLA tracking, requests pile up, priorities get lost, and tenant satisfaction plummets. The average residential manager juggles 50+ open maintenance tickets at any time.

🏮

Community Management

Amenity bookings, visitor management, community notices, parking allocation, and shared facility scheduling all need coordination. Larger communities require dedicated tools that most property managers lack.

How Darsync Solves It

Purpose-Built for Residential Properties

Every feature designed for the way residential properties actually operate in the UAE and GCC.

👤

Tenant Portal

Self-service portal where tenants submit maintenance requests, view payment history, download contracts, and communicate with management. Reduces phone calls by 60% and improves tenant satisfaction scores.

PDC Automation

Automated PDC tracking with deposit dates, bounce alerts, and WhatsApp reminders. Integrates with bank feeds for real-time reconciliation. Track every cheque across every unit from one dashboard.

Maintenance SLAs

Set response and resolution SLAs by priority level. Automatic escalation when deadlines approach. Track vendor performance, costs, and tenant satisfaction for every job. Full audit trail.

Community Engagement

Broadcast notices, manage community events, run polls, and keep residents informed. Built-in communication channels reduce complaints and build a sense of community across large developments.

Amenity Booking

Online booking for gyms, pools, BBQ areas, meeting rooms, and other shared facilities. Capacity limits, time slots, and automatic confirmations. No more double-bookings or spreadsheet chaos.
