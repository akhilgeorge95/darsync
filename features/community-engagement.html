The Problem

Tenants feel disconnected, amenity booking is chaotic, and there is no community building. Residents treat your building as just another address, not a home. When lease renewal time comes, they have no reason to stay. You are competing on price alone when you should be competing on experience.

How It Works

From Tenants to Community Members

Four steps to transform passive tenants into engaged community members.

1

Residents Join the Community

Move-in triggers an automatic invitation. Residents join the building's digital community in seconds via the app or web portal.

2

Book Amenities

Pool, gym, BBQ area, parking spots, laundry rooms — all bookable in real-time with availability calendars and automatic confirmations.

3

Participate in Events & Polls

Community events, satisfaction surveys, and decision polls. Give residents a voice in how their community is run.

4

Buy & Sell on Marketplace

Residents can trade furniture, appliances, and services within the community. Moving out? List items for your neighbours.
