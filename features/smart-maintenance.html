Smart Maintenance Management

SLA-driven work orders with intelligent auto-assignment, GPS technician tracking, and preventive scheduling. Cut response times from hours to minutes.

The Problem

Slow Response Times Are Driving Tenants Away

Slow response times, no SLA tracking, and manual assignment waste hours every day. When a tenant reports a water leak at 10 PM, your team scrambles through contact lists, makes phone calls, and hopes the right technician is available.

Without SLA tracking, you have no way to know if you are meeting your service commitments. Without data on technician performance, you cannot improve. The result: frustrated tenants, high turnover, and a reputation that hurts your occupancy rates.

2-3 Hours

Average response time with manual dispatch

How It Works

From Request to Resolution, Automatically

Darsync manages the entire maintenance lifecycle with intelligent automation at every step.

Tenant Submits Request (Portal or WhatsApp)

Tenants submit maintenance requests through the self-service portal or WhatsApp. They describe the issue, select a category, and attach photos. The request is logged instantly.

Auto-Assign to Nearest Technician

Based on the issue category, technician skills, current workload, and GPS location, Darsync automatically assigns the best-fit technician. No manual dispatching.

SLA Countdown Starts

The moment a request is logged, the SLA clock begins. Emergency (2 hours), Urgent (8 hours), Normal (24 hours). Escalation triggers automatically if deadlines approach.

Completion Verification & Feedback

When the technician marks the job complete, the tenant receives a notification to verify the work. Satisfaction feedback is collected automatically and tracked per technician.
