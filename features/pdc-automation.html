PDC Lifecycle Automation

Track every post-dated cheque from receipt to clearance. Automate deposits, handle bounces with replacement workflows, and achieve 95% auto-reconciliation.

The Problem

Spreadsheets Cannot Handle Your Cheque Volume

Manual PDC tracking in spreadsheets leads to missed deposits, bounced cheques that go unresolved, and lost revenue. With hundreds of cheques in custody at any time, one missed deposit date can cascade into cash flow problems.

When a cheque bounces, the manual process of notifying tenants, tracking penalty fees, and managing replacement cheques adds days of work. Meanwhile, reconciling cheque payments against invoices eats up your finance team's most valuable hours.

15-20%

How It Works

End-to-End PDC Lifecycle Management

From the moment a cheque is received to final clearance, Darsync manages every step automatically.

Auto-Generate PDC Schedule from Lease

When a lease is created, Darsync automatically generates the full PDC schedule based on payment terms, rent amount, and cheque frequency. No manual data entry.

Track Custody & Submission

Every cheque is tracked from the moment it is received. Know exactly which cheques are in custody, which have been submitted to the bank, and which are pending.

Auto-Deposit Reminders

Receive automated reminders before each cheque deposit date. Batch submissions by bank for efficiency. Never miss a deposit window.

Handle Bounces with Replacement Workflow

When a cheque bounces, Darsync automatically triggers the replacement workflow: notify the tenant, track penalty fees, manage replacement cheque receipt, and update the schedule.
