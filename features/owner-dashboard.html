Give property owners instant access to portfolio performance, automated monthly statements, and one-click disbursement approvals. Save 95% of report generation time.

The Problem

Owners Want Transparency, But Reports Take Days

Owners demand transparency but getting reports takes days of manual compilation. Your team spends 3-5 days every month pulling data from spreadsheets, formatting statements, and emailing PDFs to dozens of owners.

When an owner calls asking about occupancy rates or last month's collection status, your team scrambles to find the data. This erodes trust and makes owners question whether their properties are being managed effectively.

How It Works

Real-Time Transparency for Every Owner

Owners get instant access to everything they need without a single phone call to your office.

Owner Logs Into Portal

Each owner gets a secure, personalized dashboard showing their entire portfolio at a glance. Available on web and mobile with multi-language support.

See Real-Time Occupancy & Revenue

Live occupancy rates, rent collection status, outstanding balances, and revenue trends -- all updated in real-time as transactions happen.

Review Monthly Statements

Automated monthly owner statements are generated on schedule with complete income and expense breakdowns, management fees, and net proceeds.

Approve Disbursements with One Click

Owners review net proceeds and approve disbursements directly from the dashboard. Funds are released automatically upon approval.
