Ejari & GCC Compliance Integration

Automate Ejari, Tawtheeq, and DLD registration end-to-end. Cut registration time from days to minutes and never miss a compliance deadline.

The Problem

Manual Compliance Is Costing You Time and Money

Manual Ejari registration takes 2-3 days per lease. Multiply that across your portfolio and your team spends weeks every month just on paperwork. Missed deadlines mean fines from RERA, and tracking renewal dates across spreadsheets is a recipe for costly oversights.

Every missed registration or expired document puts your business at legal risk. In the GCC, compliance is not optional -- it is the foundation of property management.

How It Works

Four Steps to Automated Compliance

Darsync handles the entire compliance lifecycle so your team can focus on managing properties, not paperwork.

Auto-Detect Lease Ready for Ejari

When a lease is signed and all conditions are met, Darsync automatically flags it for Ejari registration. No manual triggers needed.

Validate All Required Documents

The system checks for all required documents -- title deed, passport copy, Emirates ID, tenancy contract -- and alerts you to anything missing before submission.

Submit Registration

With one click, submit the Ejari registration with all validated documents. Darsync formats everything to meet RERA requirements automatically.

Track Status & Renewal Deadlines

Monitor registration status in real-time and get automatic alerts at 90, 60, and 30 days before renewal deadlines. Never miss a date again.
