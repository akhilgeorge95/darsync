Tenant Experience

Tenant Self-Service Portal

Empower tenants to pay rent, submit maintenance requests, book amenities, and track everything in real-time. Reduce routine calls to your office by 90%.

The Problem

Your Team Is Drowning in Routine Tenant Requests

Tenants call or email for every request -- rent receipts, maintenance updates, amenity bookings, document copies. Each call takes 10-30 minutes of your team's time, and with hundreds of tenants, these routine inquiries consume your staff's entire day.

Meanwhile, tenants are frustrated too. They wait on hold, send emails that go unanswered for days, and have no visibility into the status of their requests. The result is dissatisfaction on both sides and high tenant turnover.

📞

30-60 Min

Average staff time per routine tenant request

How It Works

Self-Service That Tenants Actually Use

A modern portal experience that handles 90% of tenant interactions without staff involvement.

Tenant Logs In to Portal

Each tenant gets a secure login to their personalized dashboard. Available on web and mobile, with Arabic and English language support.

Pay Rent, Submit Requests, Book Amenities

Tenants can pay rent online, submit maintenance requests with photos, book shared amenities like pools and gyms, and download documents -- all self-service.

Track Request Status in Real-Time

Every request shows live status updates. Tenants see when their maintenance request is assigned, when the technician is en route, and when it is completed.

Communicate Directly with Management

Built-in messaging lets tenants communicate with management without phone calls or emails. All conversations are logged and searchable.
