Operations Suite

Complete Facility Management

Nine specialized modules covering every aspect of building operations and maintenance.

🧹

Cleaning Schedules

Create and manage cleaning schedules for common areas, lobbies, stairwells, parking, and amenities. Track completion with photo verification and supervisor sign-off. Daily, weekly, and deep-cleaning rotations.

🐛

Pest Control Scheduling

Schedule regular pest control treatments by building, floor, and unit. Track contractor visits, treatment types, and effectiveness. Automated reminders for upcoming treatments and follow-ups.

🏊

Pool Maintenance & Chemical Tracking

Log daily pool chemical readings (pH, chlorine, alkalinity). Track maintenance tasks, equipment status, and compliance with health authority standards. Automated alerts for out-of-range readings.

🚒

Fire Equipment Register & Inspection

Maintain a complete register of fire extinguishers, alarms, sprinklers, and emergency lighting. Schedule inspections, track expiry dates, and ensure Civil Defence compliance with automated alerts.

Compliance Critical
💡

Utility Meter Management

Track DEWA, water, gas, and district cooling meters. Record readings (manual or IoT-connected), calculate consumption per unit, and identify unusual usage patterns that may indicate leaks or waste.

⚡

Energy Consumption Monitoring

Monitor energy usage across your portfolio. Benchmark buildings against each other and industry standards. Identify savings opportunities and track progress toward energy reduction goals.

Sustainability
🌳

Landscaping Contracts

Manage landscaping vendor contracts, schedules, and quality standards. Track seasonal planting plans, irrigation systems, and hardscape maintenance with photo documentation.

♻️

Sustainability Metrics

Track water consumption, energy usage, waste recycling rates, and carbon footprint per building. Generate sustainability reports for owners and regulatory submissions.

ESG Reporting
📝

AMC Contractor Management

Manage Annual Maintenance Contracts with all vendors. Track contract terms, SLAs, renewal dates, and performance. Rate contractors, log visit reports, and manage warranty claims centrally.
