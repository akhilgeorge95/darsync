Use Case

Stop Using Paper Visitor Logs. Go Digital in Seconds.

Replace handwritten logbooks and phone-call confirmations with QR-based digital check-in. Residents pre-approve visitors online, and guests check in at the gate in 30 seconds.

The Problem

Paper Visitor Logs Are a Security Risk and a Time Waste

Paper logbooks are illegible, unsearchable, and easily tampered with. Guards spend 5 minutes per visitor making phone calls to confirm access. During busy hours, queues build up at the gate, frustrating residents and visitors alike. And when security needs to review who entered the building last week, good luck finding it in a stack of notebooks.

❌ The Manual Way

6 Slow, Insecure Steps

  1. Visitor arrives at gate -- Visitor stops at the security gate and waits for a guard to become available.
  2. Guard writes in logbook -- Guard manually records visitor name, ID number, vehicle plate, time, and purpose of visit in a paper logbook.
  3. Calls the resident -- Guard phones the resident's apartment or mobile to verify the visitor is expected. Often no answer on the first try.
  4. Resident confirms -- Resident picks up and verbally confirms the visitor. If they don't answer, the visitor waits or is turned away.
  5. Guard issues paper pass -- Guard fills out a paper visitor pass with unit number and expiry time. Hands it to the visitor.
  6. Visitor enters -- Visitor finally proceeds, often 5-10 minutes after arriving. Paper pass is rarely collected on exit.

Time per visitor: 5-10 minutes average

✅ The Darsync Way

3 Steps. 30 Seconds.

  1. Resident pre-approves visitor online -- Residents add expected visitors through the tenant portal or WhatsApp. They can set one-time or recurring access, specify date and time windows, and add vehicle details.
  2. Visitor shows QR code at gate -- Pre-approved visitors receive a unique QR code via WhatsApp or SMS. At the gate, the guard scans the code -- or the visitor scans at a self-service kiosk.
  3. Digital check-in in 30 seconds -- The system instantly verifies the visitor against the pre-approval, logs the entry with timestamp and photo, and notifies the resident. Check-out is tracked automatically.

Time per visitor: 30 seconds -- zero phone calls
