Use Case

Stop Missing Ejari Deadlines. Stay Compliant Automatically.

Eliminate the spreadsheet-and-portal juggle. Darsync auto-detects leases needing registration, validates documents, and tracks submission status -- reducing Ejari registration from days to minutes.

The Problem

Manual Ejari Registration Is a Compliance Nightmare

Missing an Ejari deadline means RERA fines, tenant disputes, and legal exposure. Yet most property managers still track deadlines in spreadsheets, gather documents by hand, and visit the portal manually for every single lease. With a growing portfolio, it is only a matter of time before something slips through.

❌ The Manual Way

7 Error-Prone Steps Per Lease

  1. Track deadlines in spreadsheet -- Maintain a master Excel file with every lease's Ejari expiry, renewal date, and registration status.
  2. Gather documents manually -- Chase tenants for passport copies, Emirates ID, visa pages, and trade license documents.
  3. Visit the Ejari portal -- Log in to the Ejari system, navigate the interface, and start a new registration.
  4. Fill forms field by field -- Manually enter tenant details, property details, lease terms, and payment information.
  5. Upload documents one by one -- Attach scanned documents in the correct format and size required by the portal.
  6. Pay registration fees -- Process payment through the portal and keep track of receipts.
  7. Wait and follow up -- Check back for confirmation, handle rejections, re-submit corrections if needed.

Time spent: 2-3 days per lease registration

✅ The Darsync Way

3 Automated Steps. Zero Missed Deadlines.

  1. Auto-detect leases needing registration -- Darsync continuously monitors your lease portfolio and flags any lease that needs Ejari registration, renewal, or cancellation -- with smart alerts before deadlines.
  2. Validate all documents automatically -- The system checks that all required documents are on file, in the correct format, and not expired. Missing items trigger automatic requests to tenants via WhatsApp.
  3. Submit and track status -- Registration is submitted with pre-filled data from the lease record. Track status in real-time and receive instant notifications on approval, rejection, or required corrections.

Time spent: 10 minutes per lease -- fully automated
