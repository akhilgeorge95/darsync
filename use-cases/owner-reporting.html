Use Case

Stop Compiling Reports Manually. Give Owners Real-Time Transparency.

Replace the monthly Excel scramble with a self-service owner portal. Real-time dashboards, financial statements, and disbursement reports -- accessible anytime, from anywhere.

The Problem

Owners Demand Transparency. Manual Reporting Cannot Keep Up.

Property owners expect real-time visibility into their investments. But most management companies spend 3-5 days every month pulling data from multiple systems, compiling spreadsheets, formatting reports, and emailing them out -- only to field dozens of follow-up questions. It is unsustainable as your portfolio grows.

❌ The Manual Way

6 Time-Consuming Steps Every Month

  1. Export data from multiple sources -- Pull rent collection data from one system, maintenance costs from another, and vacancy data from a third.
  2. Compile in Excel -- Merge data into a master spreadsheet, cross-reference figures, and ensure nothing is missing or duplicated.
  3. Calculate returns -- Manually compute net income, ROI, occupancy rates, and disbursement amounts for each owner.
  4. Format the report -- Create professional-looking reports with charts, tables, and summaries tailored to each owner's portfolio.
  5. Email to owners -- Send individual reports to each owner via email with PDF attachments and summary notes.
  6. Answer follow-up questions -- Spend days responding to owner emails and calls asking for clarification, additional breakdowns, or historical comparisons.

Time spent: 3-5 days per month on report generation and follow-up

✅ The Darsync Way

2 Steps. Instant Access.

  1. Owner logs into their portal -- Each owner gets a branded, secure portal with their own login. Accessible from desktop or mobile, anytime, anywhere in the world.
  2. Views real-time dashboards, statements, and disbursements -- Live financial dashboards, income statements, expense breakdowns, occupancy analytics, maintenance history, and disbursement reports -- all updated in real-time. Owners can filter by property, date range, or category and export reports on demand.

Time spent: Zero -- owners self-serve 24/7
