Use Case

Stop Chasing Rent. Start Getting Paid Automatically.

Eliminate the manual invoice-chase-follow-up cycle. Darsync automates rent collection from invoice generation to payment reconciliation -- saving your team 15+ hours every week.

The Problem

Rent Collection Shouldn't Take All Week

Most property management teams spend more time chasing payments than managing properties. Manual invoicing, WhatsApp follow-ups, spreadsheet tracking, and bank reconciliation eat up your team's most productive hours.

❌ The Manual Way

8 Painful Steps Every Month

  1. Generate invoice manually -- Open Excel, look up lease terms, calculate amounts, create invoice document one by one.
  2. Send via email or WhatsApp -- Copy-paste invoices into emails or WhatsApp messages to each tenant individually.
  3. Wait and hope -- Cross your fingers and wait for tenants to acknowledge and respond.
  4. Follow up repeatedly -- Call, message, and email tenants who haven't paid. Chase the same people every month.
  5. Receive post-dated cheques -- Physically collect PDCs from tenants, log them manually, and store them safely.
  6. Track payments in spreadsheet -- Update your master Excel sheet with who paid, who didn't, and partial payments.
  7. Deposit cheques at bank -- Make trips to the bank to deposit cheques on the correct dates.
  8. Reconcile manually -- Match bank statements to invoices, hunt for discrepancies, and update records.

Time spent: 15-20+ hours per week for a 200-unit portfolio

✅ The Darsync Way

3 Automated Steps. Zero Chasing.

  1. Auto-generate invoices -- Darsync automatically creates invoices based on lease terms, escalation clauses, and payment schedules. No manual data entry.
  2. Auto-send reminders via WhatsApp -- Automated payment reminders go out via WhatsApp, email, and SMS at configurable intervals before and after due dates.
  3. Auto-reconcile payments -- Incoming payments are matched to invoices automatically. Bank feeds sync in real-time, and outstanding balances update instantly.

Time spent: Under 1 hour per week -- fully automated
