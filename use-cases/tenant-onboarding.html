Use Case

Stop Making Move-In Day a Nightmare. Onboard Tenants in Minutes.

Replace paper forms, photocopies, and manual checklists with a fully digital onboarding experience. First impressions matter -- make move-in day seamless.

The Problem

Move-In Day Sets the Tone for the Entire Tenancy

A chaotic move-in experience -- missing documents, confusion about parking, delayed access cards, unclear community rules -- creates frustration from day one. When tenants start their lease with a negative experience, they are less likely to renew. Yet most property managers still rely on paper forms and manual checklists that take 1-2 days to complete.

❌ The Manual Way

8 Disorganized Steps

  1. Collect documents manually -- Ask tenants to bring passport copies, Emirates ID, visa, trade license, and other documents on move-in day.
  2. Photocopy IDs -- Make physical photocopies of every document, often with a queue of waiting tenants.
  3. Create tenant file -- Assemble a physical folder with all documents, signed lease, and tenant information form.
  4. Assign parking and storage -- Check availability, assign spaces, and update the allocation spreadsheet manually.
  5. Issue access cards -- Program access cards for the building, apartment, gym, and pool. Track card numbers in another spreadsheet.
  6. Explain community rules -- Walk the tenant through building rules, noise policies, pet regulations, and waste disposal guidelines verbally.
  7. Give emergency contacts -- Provide phone numbers for security, maintenance, and management on a printed sheet.
  8. Update master records -- Go back to the office and update the tenant database, parking records, and access card logs.

Time spent: 1-2 days per tenant onboarding

✅ The Darsync Way

3 Steps. Welcome Ready.

  1. Tenant completes digital onboarding -- Before move-in day, tenants receive a digital onboarding link via WhatsApp. They upload documents, sign forms electronically, acknowledge community rules, and provide emergency contacts -- all from their phone.
  2. Auto-assign amenities and access -- Darsync automatically assigns parking spots, storage units, and gym/pool access based on lease entitlements. Access card details are pre-programmed and linked to the tenant profile.
  3. Welcome kit sent automatically -- On move-in day, tenants receive a digital welcome kit with building guide, emergency contacts, community rules, Wi-Fi details, and move-in checklist -- all via WhatsApp and email.

Time spent: 10 minutes -- everything happens before they arrive
