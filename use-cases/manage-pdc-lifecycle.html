Use Case

Stop Tracking Cheques in Spreadsheets. Automate the Entire PDC Lifecycle.

From cheque collection to bank deposit to bounce handling -- Darsync automates every step of post-dated cheque management. Zero missed deposits, 95% auto-reconciliation.

The Problem

PDC Management Is Still Stuck in the 1990s

In the GCC, post-dated cheques remain the dominant payment method for rent. Yet most property managers track hundreds or thousands of cheques in Excel, store them in filing cabinets, and manually submit them to the bank on deposit dates. One missed cheque means lost revenue. One bounced cheque means weeks of follow-up.

❌ The Manual Way

7 Risk-Filled Steps

  1. Receive post-dated cheques -- Collect physical cheques from tenants during lease signing or renewal, verify details match the lease.
  2. Log in spreadsheet -- Manually enter cheque number, amount, date, tenant name, and property into your master tracking sheet.
  3. Store cheques physically -- File cheques in envelopes or binders organized by deposit date. Hope nothing gets lost or misplaced.
  4. Track deposit dates -- Check the spreadsheet daily to identify which cheques need to be submitted to the bank this week.
  5. Submit to bank manually -- Physically take cheques to the bank branch or prepare deposit slips for courier collection.
  6. Check if cleared -- Monitor bank statements over the next few days to confirm each cheque has cleared.
  7. Handle bounces manually -- When a cheque bounces, call the tenant, request a replacement, track the new cheque, and restart the process.

Risk level: High -- missed deposits, lost cheques, and untracked bounces are common

✅ The Darsync Way

3 Steps. Complete Control.

  1. Auto-generate PDC schedule from lease -- When a lease is created, Darsync automatically generates the full cheque schedule with amounts, dates, and cheque numbers. No manual data entry.
  2. Auto-track custody and submission -- Digital custody tracking from collection to bank deposit. Automated reminders for upcoming deposits and real-time status updates across the entire PDC portfolio.
  3. Auto-handle bounces with replacement workflow -- Bounced cheques trigger automatic tenant notifications via WhatsApp, replacement cheque requests, and tracking of the new cheque through the full lifecycle.

Risk level: Zero -- every cheque tracked, every deposit on time
